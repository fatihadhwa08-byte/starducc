About Our Airline

Starducc Airlines commits to safe, on-time, and modern flight experiences for every passenger. Operating proudly from Yogyakarta International Airport (YIA), we utilize eco-friendly, next-gen fleets to actively minimize carbon emissions while maintaining our signature global-tier hospitality.

We believe that premium comfort and environmental responsibility can seamlessly go hand in hand.
